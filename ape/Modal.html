Android App Development :

ACM NIT Surat's Android App

This project aims at building an android app for the chapter, having the following features:

  1. Details (area of expertise) and contact info of the current and previous committee members
  2. Implementation of a notification system of upcoming events and workshops
  3. A suggestions tab where students can suggest events and workshops and give ideas on improving ACM
  4. A query page where students can ask questions related to workshops, events, or any programming concepts in general.


Free To All New Ideas!!!

Pre-requisites: Basic knowledge of Android and Java.
Difficulty: Medium
Mentor(s): Jay Panchal, jaypanchal33@yahoo.com
